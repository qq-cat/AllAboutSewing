# Pattern Making — Notes

## Fundamentals

- **Grain line** — direction of fabric grain; affects drape and stretch
- **Seam allowance** — extra fabric beyond the stitch line (typically 1–1.5 cm)
- **Ease** — extra room beyond body measurements for comfort and movement
