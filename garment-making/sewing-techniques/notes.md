# Sewing Techniques — Notes

## Essential Stitches

| Stitch | Use |
|---|---|
| Straight stitch | Main construction seam |
| Zigzag | Finishing raw edges, knit fabric |
| Backstitch | Hand sewing, reinforcing ends |
| Basting | Temporary stitch for fitting |

---

## Seam Finishing

- **Serger / Overlock** — fastest, cleanest finish
- **Zigzag** — alternative without a serger
- **French seam** — enclosed, clean on both sides; for sheer fabrics
- **Flat-felled seam** — very strong; used in denim

## Darts

Darts shape flat fabric to body curves (bust, waist, back).

- Mark with tailor's chalk or tracing paper
- Stitch from wide end to point
- Press toward center seam or downward

## Closures

- **Zippers** — invisible, lapped, centered
- **Buttons & buttonholes**
- **Snaps / hooks & eyes**

---

## Pressing Tips

- Press seams open or to one side as you go — don't wait until the end
- Use a tailor's ham for curved seams
- Steam is your friend for shaping
