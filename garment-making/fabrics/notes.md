# Fabrics — Notes

## Fabric Categories

### Woven
- Threads interlocked at 90° — stable, doesn't stretch on grain
- Examples: cotton, linen, silk, denim, chiffon

### Knit
- Looped construction — stretches, good recovery
- Examples: jersey, rib knit, interlock

### Non-woven
- Bonded fibers — interfacing, felt

## Fabric Prep

- **Pre-wash** before cutting to prevent shrinkage
- **Press** before cutting for accuracy
- Check grain line before laying pattern pieces
