📐 Body Measurements

Accurate measurements are the foundation of a well-fitting garment. Take them wearing form-fitting undergarments, standing naturally, with a helper if possible.

💡 Golden rule: keep the tape measure snug but not tight — you should be able to slip one finger underneath it.

What You Need

📏 Flexible tape measure
🖊️ Pen and paper (or phone)
🪞 Full-length mirror
🧍 A helper (ideal)
👙 Form-fitting undergarments

How to take each measurement

Ref 中文 / English How to measure Used for
Circumferences 圍
A
 胸圍
Bust		 Wrap tape around the fullest part of the chest, parallel to the floor. Breathe normally — don't expand or contract. Tops, jackets, dresses
B
 腰圍
Waist		 Find natural waist by bending side to side — it's the crease that forms. Slightly tighter for skirts/trousers; looser for qipao. All garments
C
 腹圍
Abdomen		 Measure below the navel, at the fullest point between the waist and hip. Trousers, skirts
D
 坐圍
Hip		 Stand with feet together. Measure around fullest part of hips and seat (~18–23 cm below natural waist). Leave a little ease. Skirts, trousers, dresses
Widths 寬
E
 肩寬
Shoulder		 Measure across the back from left shoulder point to right shoulder point (where shoulder seam sits). Best taken with a helper. Tops, jackets, coats
F
 前寬
Front		 Across the front chest from left underarm to right underarm, measured about 10 cm below the shoulder seam. Bodice, blouses
— 後寬
Back		 Across the back from left underarm to right underarm at the same level as front width. Bodice, jackets
G
 乳寬
Breast Distance		 Horizontal distance from nipple to nipple (bust span). Dart placement, Clo3D avatar
— 腋寬
Arm Hole		 Measure around the armhole opening — from the front underarm point, over the shoulder, back to underarm. Sleeves, armhole shaping
— 領寬 / 領高
Collar W / H		 Collar width = distance across the collar base. Collar height = how tall the collar stands. Collar construction
Lengths 直
H
 胸高
Breast Length		 From shoulder (at neckline) straight down to the bust point (nipple level). Measured vertically down the front. Dart placement, bodice
I
 腰長
Waist Length		 From the most prominent vertebra at the nape of the neck straight down the center back to the natural waist. Bodice blocks, dresses
J
 臀長
Hip Length		 From shoulder (or nape) down to the fullest hip point. Also recorded as the distance from waist to fullest hip. Skirts, dresses
— 衫長
Blouse Length		 From shoulder seam at neckline down to desired blouse hem. Blouses, tops
— 裙長 / 半截裙長
Dress / Skirt Length		 From shoulder to desired hem for dresses; from waist to desired hem for skirts. Dresses, skirts
Sleeves 袖
K
 袖長
Sleeve Length		 From shoulder point, over bent elbow, to 1 inch below the wrist bone. Sleeves
— 袖山 / 袖大
Arm / Sleeve Width		 Arm width (袖山) = fullest part of upper arm. Sleeve width (袖大) = desired width at the sleeve cap. Sleeve cap drafting
— 袖口
Cuff		 Circumference of the wrist; add ease for cuff style (tight = +2 cm, loose = +5 cm). Cuffs, sleeve hems
Pants 褲
— 祆頭
Waist Width		 Width of the trouser waistband at center front. Waistband
— 比寬
Thigh		 Circumference of the fullest part of the upper thigh. Trousers
— 浪深
Crotch		 Sit on a hard chair. Measure from side waist down to the seat of the chair. Trousers, shorts
M
 祆長
Outseam		 From natural waist, down the outer side of the leg to the floor. Trousers, skirts
L
 袴長
Inseam		 From crotch seam (inner thigh) down to the floor. Easier to measure on a well-fitting pair of trousers. Trousers, jeans
— 腳寬
Pant Ankle		 Desired width of the trouser leg at the ankle hem. Trouser hem
